What is stuyCTF?

stuyCTF is a capture-the-flag competition designed and built by Stuyvesant High School Students for high schoolers in general. This is inspired by various other competitions such as EasyCTF, HSCTF etc. The competition is designed to introduce high schoolers (especially Stuyvesant students) to hacking and other cyber security fields. For more information about the general idea of ctf's and this competition, please refer to our FAQ page.
