What is stuyCTF?

stuyCTF is an online jeopardy-style Capture the Flag competition. Featuring problems that range from Binary and Web Exploitations to Reverse Engineering and Forensics, stuyCTF is the ultimate CTF competition to get both new and veteran competitors excited about computer science and security. It serves as an excellent hands-on experience that is both rewarding and fun!
