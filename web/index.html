What is stuyCTF?

stuyCTF is an online jeopardy-style Capture the Flag competition where the objective is to solve problems to obtain "flags". Featuring problems that range from Binary and Web Exploitations to Reverse Engineering and Forensics, stuyCTF is the ultimate high school CTF competition to get both new and veteran competitors excited about computer science and security. It serves as an excellent hands-on experience that is both rewarding and fun!
